Memories in Tassie


Welcome to Xia Yu's exhibition of Tasmania Photography!

Introduction: This Gallery is about eh Photography works that Xia Yu took in Tasmania. Xia Yu hopes that viewers can enjoy the photos and also get some experience of trip in Tasmania.

Launceston

This photo was taken on my way from Launceston to Hobart. The city of Launceston gave me a very comfortable feeling. When I left, I was very reluctant, so I sat in the car and took the photo. Scaled up the width of the photo to make it look like a painting.

Queenstown


Took these three pictures in the morning in Queenstown, I had breakfast at the only restaurant in town that is open very early and took picture two. At that time, the sky was taking a walk in the streets of the town in the drizzle to take the first picture, and the third picture was taken with a drone, looking at Queenstown from the sky.

Night Road


This photo was taken at night with a long exposure. There are dense stars in the sky. When I finished taking the photo, I was shocked by the clean land on the ground, echoing the pure starry sky, and there is a road leading to the distance in the middle.

Milkyway Photography

The content of this picture is, starry sky and fields, which is the ideal living environment I have been chasing.Although the weather was cold during the shooting, I was grateful to be very free and comfortable because I found the place of my dream.
